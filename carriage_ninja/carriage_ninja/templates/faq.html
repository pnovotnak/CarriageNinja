FAQs

What can I ship?

In general, if it doesn't pose a risk to the driver, need an MSDS, isn't an illicit substance, and isn't worth a lot. This is just a bulletin board, and we don't take responsibility for what you decide to ship, but the law might. Don't be stupid.

How does payment work?

For now, payment is handled by users. We don't exchange money.

What shouldn't I ship?

In general, shipping high-value items is discouraged. You should let a professional service ship those.
